Current
Research focus

Quantifying population immunity

Measuring population immunity remains a persistent challenge. My research integrates routine data streams to quantify it, and to understand how immunity and transmission together shape outbreak risk.

The long-term goal: build sustainable surveillance that stays low cost but effective, so public health can move from monitoring the past to forecasting future immunity and guiding proactive response.
