Research interests

My research focuses on quantifying population immunity and understanding how immunity and transmission shape outbreak risk. In parallel, I advise policymakers in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam on designing resilient and sustainable surveillance systems, and train an in-house modelling team for the government to strengthen decision-making and outbreak response.
